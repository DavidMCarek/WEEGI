to debug the android app on your phone instead of on an emulator. 

first go to your phones settings.

developer options need to be enabled. 
to do this go to  'about phone' 
tap on 'build number' 7 timmes 

tap the back button and you should now see 'developer options' 
go into this setting and allow USB debugging 

and boom now you can debug on your own phone when you are plugged into your computer!
